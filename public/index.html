  • The hide and seek event has ended. It is now replaced by Kill Race event!
    • On the Kill Race event, there is a huge labyrinth which you have to reach the green zone to win.
  • There is a timer of 60 seconds. Once the timer runs out, the brown wall gets destroyed and you can complete the labyrinth.
    • After the destruction of the wall, a timer of 3 minutes will start which will be the killer's apparition.
  • When the 3 minutes are passed, you can no longer respawn back and a huge killer appears on the base and you will have to hurry up. You will have 3 more minutes to reach the green zone.
  • If you still didn't reach the green zone after the 3 minutes or all your teammates die by the killer, you lose!
  • Completely reworked the UI and it's now similar to diep.io
  • Next update is going to be the mobile support.
